## Part 2: Pricing Strategy — Per-Student vs Flat Tiers

### My Recommendation: **Hybrid Per-Student Pricing**

Per-student pricing is **significantly better** for you than flat tiers. Here's why:

| Factor | Flat Tiers (Current) | Per-Student (Recommended) |
|---|---|---|
| Revenue growth | Only grows when you add new institutes | Grows automatically as institutes add students |
| Fairness | Small institute with 20 students pays same as one with 150 | Pays proportional to value received |
| Net Revenue Retention | ~100% (flat) | **110-130%** (expansion revenue — key for valuation) |
| Upsell friction | "Upgrade your plan" = awkward conversation | Automatic — more students = more revenue |
| Investor appeal | Basic SaaS | **Usage-based growth** = higher valuation multiple |

### Recommended Pricing Structure

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                     FREE TIER                           │
│  Up to 10 students · 1 batch · Basic features           │
│  No WhatsApp · No biometric · 3-month trial of Pro      │
│  Purpose: Get them hooked, parents download the app      │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                    STARTER — ₹25/student/mo              │
│  Up to 50 students · 5 batches · Push notifications      │
│  Min: ₹499/mo (even if <20 students)                    │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                    GROWTH — ₹35/student/mo               │
│  Up to 200 students · 20 batches · WhatsApp alerts       │
│  Data import · Timetable · Report cards                  │
│  Min: ₹1,499/mo                                         │
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                   SCALE — ₹30/student/mo                 │
│  Unlimited students · Unlimited batches                  │
│  WhatsApp + Biometric · Priority support                 │
│  Min: ₹4,999/mo                                         │
│  (Lower per-student = volume discount incentive)         │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Why This Works

**Example: An institute with 80 students on Growth plan**
- Monthly bill: 80 × ₹35 = **₹2,800/mo**
- They add 30 more students next quarter: 110 × ₹35 = **₹3,850/mo**
- Your revenue grew **37% without doing anything** ← This is "Net Revenue Retention > 100%"
- Investors LOVE this metric

**Example: Small new tuition center with 15 students**
- Starts on FREE tier (10 students)
- Upgrades to Starter when they hit 11: 15 × ₹25 = **₹499/mo** (hits minimum)
- Zero risk for them. They only pay when they're getting value.

## Part 3: Free Tier / Trial Strategy

### What to Offer Free

> [!TIP]
> **The golden rule: Make the free tier addictive enough that parents download the app. Once parents have the app, the institute can never leave.**

| Feature | Free Tier | Paid |
|---|---|---|
| Students | Up to **10** | Unlimited |
| Batches | 1 | Unlimited |
| Parent App | ✅ Full access | ✅ |
| Attendance | ✅ (push only) | ✅ + WhatsApp |
| Report Cards | ✅ | ✅ |
| Fee Tracking | ✅ Basic | ✅ + Reminders |
| WhatsApp Alerts | ❌ | ✅ (Growth+) |
| Biometric | ❌ | ✅ (Scale only) |
| Data Import | ❌ | ✅ |
| Support | Community | Priority |
| Duration | **Forever** (not trial) | — |

### Why "Forever Free" Beats "3-Month Trial"

| Strategy | Pros | Cons |
|---|---|---|
| **3-6 month free trial** | Creates urgency | If they don't convert, you lose them entirely |
| **Forever free + 10 student cap** | Never churns, always a potential convert, word-of-mouth | Some stay free forever (but they're marketing for you) |

**Recommended: Forever free up to 10 students** + **3-month free trial of Growth features** (WhatsApp, import, timetable)

This way:
1. They sign up free → import 10 students → parents download app
2. For 3 months, they experience WhatsApp alerts and full features
3. After 3 months, WhatsApp stops → they feel the pain → they upgrade
4. Even if they don't upgrade, they stay on free tier = **referral source**

### Conversion Triggers (What Makes Them Upgrade)

| Trigger | What Happens |
|---|---|
| **11th student** | "You've reached the free limit. Upgrade to Starter for ₹499/mo" |
| **WhatsApp trial expires** | Parents ask "Why did the messages stop?" → institute feels pressure |
| **End of month** | "You had 3 absent alerts this month that parents didn't see on WhatsApp" |
| **Fee reminder** | "5 parents have overdue fees. Auto-remind them on WhatsApp? Upgrade." |

## Part 6: The Moat — Why They Can Never Leave

Your biggest competitive advantage is **switching cost**. Here's how to deepen it every quarter:

| Quarter | Lock-In Feature | Switching Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Q1 | Parents download the app | Parents would need to install a new app |
| Q2 | 6+ months of attendance data | Historical data is irreplaceable |
| Q3 | Biometric device configured | Hardware tied to your API |
| Q4 | Fee payment history | Financial records can't be migrated easily |
| Q5 | Test results & analytics | 1 year of trend data = goldmine |
| Q6 | Parents habituated to WhatsApp alerts | Parents complain if alerts stop |
| Q7 | Annual report cards generated | "We've always used Cosmos" |
| Q8 | Multi-branch data | If they have 2+ locations, migration is 10x harder |

> [!TIP]
> **Every month a customer stays, their switching cost increases.** After 12 months, switching to a competitor would mean losing all historical data, re-installing apps for all parents, re-configuring biometric devices, and retraining staff. This is why SaaS companies with high retention get premium valuations.
